Stage Before Fight

What to do before summoning

  • Break 3 Shadow Orbs — each drops loot (Musket, Vilethorn, or Shadow Orb mount). 3rd orb spawns EoW
  • Craft Worm Food — Rotten Chunk ×15 + Vile Powder ×30 at Demon Altar
  • Scout the terrain — find flat area in Corruption or build arena

Gear

Armor

  • Platinum — best available
  • Fossil — good for ranged
  • Meteor + Space Gun — easy mode

Weapons (Piercing)

  • Vilethorn — through walls, all segments
  • Jester's Arrows — pierce entire worm
  • Grenades — 3-4 destroy a clump
  • Musket — slow but hits hard
  • Water Bolt — bounces, melts

Arena Setup

  • 2-3 rows of platforms spaced 6-8 blocks apart
  • Campfire — regen boost
  • Heart Lantern — +1 HP/s
  • Star in a Bottle — mana regen
  • Sunflower — +10% speed

Strategy

Phase 1 — Intact worm

  • Shoot vertically down through platforms
  • Vilethorn cuts every segment in range
  • Jester's Arrows = best ranged piercing

Phase 2 — Splits into smaller worms

  • Don't run — kill small worms before they regroup
  • Grenades one-shot small segments
  • Focus segment clumps

Pro tips

  • EoW can't go through solid blocks
  • Musket: aim for the head
  • Bring 200+ ammo without piercing
  • With piercing: 50-80 shots enough
